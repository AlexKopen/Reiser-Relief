Make a Difference

Our mission trips make a real, tangible difference in the lives of those we serve. The objective of each trip varies based on the needs of our ministries and mission partners in Haiti. We work at schools, orphanages, homes for the elderly and disabled, and hospitals. Most importantly, we share the Gospel by being Christ's hands and feet through our service to the poorest of the poor.

Leave Your Comfort Zone

Mission work is intense. You will be required to leave the comfort of your everyday life and embrace unfamiliar places and situations. The experience will challenge your worldview and cause you to examine your heart and priorities. At the same time, you will learn from Haiti's vast history and beautiful culture. You will discover that the mission week is a period of enormous personal growth and development.
